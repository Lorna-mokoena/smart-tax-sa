# Smart Tax SA 🇿🇦

Free South African tax calculators, payroll tools and financial guides designed to help individuals, freelancers and small businesses estimate taxes quickly and easily.

## Tools Included

### PAYE Calculator
Estimate employee PAYE deductions and net salary.

### Income Tax Calculator
Calculate estimated income tax using South African tax brackets.

### UIF Calculator
Estimate UIF contributions and deductions.

### Bonus Tax Calculator
Estimate tax impact on bonuses and incentive payments.

### Retirement Tax Calculator
Estimate retirement contribution tax savings.
